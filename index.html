The Concept

Most of the animals on Planet Earth sleep at night and wake up naturally at dawn. Human society has been forcing a different system on itself and it's not natural, healthy, or necessary.

☀

Sunrise-Aligned

Zero Hour tracks the natural day. When the Sun comes up, the parallel clock starts. ZHT 0 is dawn-adjacent, making the named start of the day match visible daylight.

⇄

Parallel System

Ordinary clock time remains unchanged for transport, software, law, aviation, and finance. Zero Hour exists alongside it as a human-centered schedule layer.

🌡

Smooth Seasonal Drift

Instead of the abrupt one-hour shock of daylight saving time, Zero Hour drifts continuously with the seasons, following the Sun's natural motion.

📅

Natural Scheduling

Daily life can be expressed in Zero Time. "School starts at ZHT 1.5" means one and a half hours after sunrise. Shops open at ZHT 2. Intuitive and universal.

💻

Zero Disruption

No technical system needs rewriting. Zero Hour preserves civil time completely. It's additive, not a replacement — a second time scale for human routines.

🌎

Fully Predictable

Zero Hour is computed from date, latitude, longitude, and a chosen dawn offset. It's deterministic for any location on Earth, for any date, past or future.

How It Works

A mathematically clean definition anchored to local sunrise, with an optional offset for practical use.

Core Definition

ZeroHourLocal(date, lat, lng) = LocalSunrise(date, lat, lng) + k

Where k is a chosen offset in minutes. k = 0 means zero hour is sunrise exactly. k = +20 means zero hour occurs 20 minutes after sunrise.

Solar Calculation

γ = (2π / 365) × (N − 1 + (h − 12) / 24) E = 229.18 × (0.000075 + 0.001868·cos γ − 0.032077·sin γ − 0.014615·cos 2γ − 0.040849·sin 2γ) δ = 0.006918 − 0.399912·cos γ + 0.070257·sin γ − 0.006758·cos 2γ + 0.000907·sin 2γ − 0.002697·cos 3γ + 0.00148·sin 3γ cos H = cos(90.833°) / (cos φ · cos δ) − tan φ · tan δ Sunrise (UTC min) = 720 − 4 × (λ + H) − E

N = day of year, h = approximate sunrise hour (6), φ = latitude, δ = solar declination, λ = longitude (degrees east), E = equation of time (minutes), H = hour angle (degrees). This is the standard NOAA solar position model.

Converting Civil Time to Zero Hour Time

ZHT(t) = (t − ZeroHourLocal) / 60 ZHT₂₄(t) = ((t − ZeroHourLocal) / 60) mod 24

Where t is current UTC time in minutes after midnight and ZeroHourLocal is sunrise UTC minutes + offset k. The result is hours in Zero Hour Time.

Zero Hour vs Daylight Saving

Why continuous seasonal drift beats abrupt legislative clock changes.

Daylight Saving Time

  • Abrupt one-hour jump twice a year
  • Disrupts sleep schedules and circadian rhythms
  • Causes measurable spikes in accidents and health events
  • Legislative and politically contentious
  • Disconnected from actual solar position

Zero Hour

  • Continuous, smooth seasonal adjustment
  • Follows the Sun's natural motion
  • No sudden shocks to biological rhythms
  • Deterministic formula, not legislative policy
  • Dawn-adjacent by definition
